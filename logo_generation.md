# Knee-Care Logo Generation

This file documents the generation of the Knee-Care app logo.

## Prompt used for Gemini 2.5 Flash Image:
"A clean, minimal, and professional app logo for a knee-care application. The logo features a stylized, modern illustration of a human knee joint with a clearly visible, sleek knee brace or support wrap securely around it. The design is medical and professional, using a soft health-themed color palette of emerald green, light blue, and clean white. No full human body or face is shown, focusing entirely on the knee joint and the support wrap. The style is high-clarity, realistic yet modern, and optimized to work perfectly as a small app icon. No text is included in the logo."
